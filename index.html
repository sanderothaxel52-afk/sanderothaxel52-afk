How to Play

Buy AIs from the market to generate cash each day. Each AI uses power and may add heat. Upgrade your base for more capacity. Raid to try stealing stronger AIs, but failed raids increase heat. If heat gets too high, enforcement may seize some of your assets.

  • Run 1 Day to earn passive income.
  • Buy strong AIs and balance power usage.
  • Use raids for high-risk upgrades.
  • Keep heat under control or lose progress.
